Export Control Guidance 042

Licensing Requirements for Advanced Semiconductors — ECCN 3A090

Issuing Authority: Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS), U.S. Department of Commerce

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Published: April 28, 2026

Overview

This guidance clarifies licensing requirements for the export, reexport, and in-country transfer of advanced integrated circuits controlled under Export Administration Regulations (EAR) ECCN 3A090.

Controlled Items

  • ECCN 3A090.a — Integrated circuits with performance density exceeding 4800 TOPS
  • ECCN 3A090.b — Systems containing such integrated circuits

Licensing Policy

A license is required for export to all destinations listed in Country Group D:1 and D:5 of Supplement No. 1 to Part 740 of the EAR. License applications will be reviewed under a presumption of denial for destinations in Country Group D:5.

Compliance Deadline

Exporters currently operating under License Exception technology and software — unrestricted (TSU) must re-evaluate their classification by April 30, 2026 and apply for the appropriate license if required.

Penalties

Violations of the EAR may result in civil penalties up to $364,992 per violation or twice the value of the transaction, and criminal penalties up to $1,000,000 and 20 years imprisonment.
