Customs Ruling 2026-015

HTS Classification: Electric Vehicle Battery Packs

Issuing Authority: U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP)

Ruling Date: April 25, 2026

Port of Entry: Port of Los Angeles

Issue

Whether complete electric vehicle (EV) battery packs, consisting of multiple lithium-ion cells, a battery management system (BMS), and a thermal management unit, are classified under HTS 8507.60 or HTS 8507.80.

Ruling

CBP rules that complete EV battery packs as described are classified under HTS 8507.60.00 (lithium-ion accumulators), dutiable at the column 1 general rate of 3.4% ad valorem.

Rationale

The battery management system and thermal management unit are considered integral parts of the lithium-ion accumulator and do not change its essential character. GRI 3(b) applies; the complete pack is classified by the component that gives it its essential character.

Binding Effect

This ruling is binding on CBP personnel and may be relied upon by the importer for a period of three years from the date of issue, unless modified or revoked.

Required Documentation

  • Commercial invoice specifying cell chemistry and BMS specifications
  • Certificate of origin (Form CO-2026)
  • Technical data sheet confirming lithium-ion chemistry
