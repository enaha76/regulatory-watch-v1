Tariff Notice 2026-001

Additional Duties on Lithium Battery Imports — People's Republic of China

Issuing Authority: Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR)

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Published: April 28, 2026

Summary

Pursuant to Section 301 of the Trade Act of 1974, the USTR hereby imposes additional ad valorem duties of 25% on imports of lithium-ion batteries and battery components originating from the People's Republic of China.

Affected HTS Codes

  • HTS 8507.60.00 — Lithium-ion accumulators
  • HTS 8507.90.80 — Parts of electric accumulators
  • HTS 8548.10.00 — Spent primary cells and batteries

Compliance Requirements

All importers of record must:

  1. File updated entry documentation reflecting the additional 25% duty by May 15, 2026.
  2. Ensure certificates of origin are on file for all shipments departing after June 1, 2026.
  3. Report any inventory in transit as of the effective date to CBP within 30 days.
  4. NEW: Submit a Duty Impact Declaration (Form CBP-2026-D) no later than May 31, 2026 for all open purchase orders placed before April 28, 2026.

Penalties

Failure to comply may result in penalties of up to $10,000 per violation under 19 U.S.C. § 1592. Repeat violations may result in seizure of goods.
